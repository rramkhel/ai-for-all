Program Overview

The "AI for All" program transforms the perceived risks and costs of AI into strategic advantages through a phased, accessible, and strategic roadmap designed specifically for St. Albert's small and medium-sized businesses.

Foundation Phase

Build confidence and literacy with AI Fundamentals, governance frameworks, and prompt engineering basics. Learn to prevent costly mistakes from day one.

Tools & Application

Master practical, low-cost AI tools for marketing, productivity, and business insights. Get immediate ROI with hands-on training and take-home resources.

Sector Deep Dives

Specialized training for Retail & E-commerce, Professional Services, and Manufacturing & Agri-Tech sectors with industry-specific applications.
