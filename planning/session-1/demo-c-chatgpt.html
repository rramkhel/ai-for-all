Document 1: Brand Voice Guide

EVERGREEN RENOS — Brand Voice Guide


Who We Are:
Family-owned renovation company in St. Albert, AB. Est. 2018. Specializing in kitchen and bathroom renovations for homeowners.


Our Voice:

• Warm but professional. We're the neighbour you trust with your house keys.
• No jargon. "We'll replace your countertops" not "countertop substrate remediation."
• Always mention our warranty and local roots.


Key Differentiators:

• 5-year workmanship warranty (longest in St. Albert)
• All crews are our employees, not subcontractors
• We clean up every day — your family still lives there


Target Customer:
Homeowners aged 35-55 in St. Albert, Morinville, Spruce Grove. Household income $100K–$180K. Usually triggered by: "We've been meaning to do this for years."

EVERGREEN RENOS — Services & Pricing 2026
