How AI Actually Works

A few technical descriptions (painless, promise)

1
GenAI vs. "AI"

Generative AI (what we're using) creates new content. It's a subset of "AI" ‚Äî which includes everything from spam filters to self-driving cars.

2
The Pipeline: Ingestion ‚Üí Tokens ‚Üí Embeddings

Text gets broken into tokens (numbers), then placed on a map where similar concepts cluster together.

3
The Transformer (The Crucial Part)

"The bank was closed..." ‚Äî River bank or money bank? The transformer reads the whole sentence to figure out which one.

üê± Visual Cue: The model knows "cat" and "kitten" are close ‚Äî and that "refrigerator" is far away.
