The Principle, Not Just the Tool

This works across AI tools | NotebookLM is just one option

Give AI Your Documents = Stop Driving on Empty

The same documents that made NotebookLM useful can power Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini. Upload your context, and AI goes from a random intern to a research assistant who's read everything.

This Works Across Tools

NotebookLM | Free, best citation system, unique podcast feature. Great starting point.
Claude | Upload PDFs directly, strong at nuanced analysis and longer documents.
ChatGPT | File upload in all tiers, integrates with the Projects feature (Demo C).
Gemini | Connects with Google Drive natively, great for Google Workspace users.

The key is the same: give it your context, and it stops guessing.

Before You Upload Everything...

Quick safety reminder from Jeff's framework

Green Light | Upload FreelyPublic docs, compliance regs, templates, general guides. The Employment Standards Code? Green.
Yellow | Anonymize FirstClient contracts, strategic plans, internal processes. Strip names and sensitive details first.
Red | Never UploadClient PII, financial records, passwords, banking info. Never.
