🔨 Construction: Client Follow-Up Email

Role: You are a professional residential renovation contractor

Task: Write a follow-up email for a client who received a kitchen renovation quote 5 days ago but hasn't responded

Context: My company does kitchen and bathroom renovations in St. Albert, AB. Our clients are homeowners, typically 35–60 years old. The quote was in the $40K–$50K range. The client seemed enthusiastic at the walk-through but mentioned they were comparing a few companies.

Format: Under 150 words. Friendly but professional. Include a soft deadline — mention our spring schedule is filling up. End with a clear call to action.

Safety Check: ✅ No real names, no exact dollar amount — this is GREEN zone.

Assembled Prompt:

**Role:** You are a professional residential renovation contractor **Task:** Write a follow-up email for a client who received a kitchen renovation quote 5 days ago but hasn't responded **Context:** My company does kitchen and bathroom renovations in St. Albert, AB. Our clients are homeowners, typically 35–60 years old. The quote was in the $40K–$50K range. The client seemed enthusiastic at the walk-through but mentioned they were comparing a few companies. **Format:** Under 150 words. Friendly but professional. Include a soft deadline — mention our spring schedule is filling up. End with a clear call to action.

🛍️ Retail: Social Media Post

Role: You are a social media marketing specialist

Task: Create 3 Instagram post ideas for a Mother's Day promotion

Context: I run a boutique gift shop. Our customers are mostly women aged 30–55 who value unique, locally-made products. We're promoting a curated Mother's Day gift box ($65–$85).

Format: For each post: a caption under 100 words, a suggested image description, and 3 relevant hashtags. Voice: warm, genuine, not salesy.

Safety Check: ✅ No customer data, general product info — this is GREEN zone.

Assembled Prompt:

**Role:** You are a social media marketing specialist **Task:** Create 3 Instagram post ideas for a Mother's Day promotion **Context:** I run a boutique gift shop. Our customers are mostly women aged 30–55 who value unique, locally-made products. We're promoting a curated Mother's Day gift box ($65–$85). **Format:** For each post: a caption under 100 words, a suggested image description, and 3 relevant hashtags. Voice: warm, genuine, not salesy.

💼 Professional Services: Meeting Summary

Role: You are a professional business consultant

Task: Turn these rough meeting notes into a clear action item list

Context: Small accounting firm (6 people). Meeting was about migrating from desktop QuickBooks to cloud-based accounting software. Three options were discussed.

Format: Numbered list. Each item: what needs to happen, who's responsible, and a suggested deadline. Keep to one page.

Safety Check: ✅ No client names or financial details included — this is GREEN zone. If I were including client info, I'd anonymize it first (YELLOW zone).

Assembled Prompt:

1

Set the Role

Tell the AI who it should "be" for this task

e.g., "an experienced marketing copywriter", "a professional accountant", "a patient teacher"

💡 Examples

You are an experienced construction estimator

You are a friendly customer service representative

You are a professional business writer

2

Define the Task

What do you want the AI to do?

Be specific about what you want created or accomplished

💡 Examples

Write a follow-up email for a customer who requested a quote but hasn't responded in a week

Create a checklist for onboarding a new employee in a retail store

Summarize these meeting notes into 5 key action items

3

Provide Context

Background info the AI needs to do a good job

Industry, audience, situation, constraints, etc.

💡 Examples

My business is a residential renovation company. Our customers are homeowners aged 35-60 in St. Albert.

This is for a professional audience who prefers formal communication.

The customer seemed interested but mentioned budget concerns.

4

Specify Format

How should the output be structured?

Length, structure, tone, any specific requirements

💡 Examples

Keep it under 150 words, professional but warm tone

Bullet points, no more than 10 items

A table with columns for Task, Owner, and Due Date

💡 Pro Tips for Better Prompts

🎯

Be specific: "Write a 100-word product description" beats "Write something about my product"

📝

Show examples: Include a sample of the style or format you want: "Like this: [example]"

🔄

Iterate: First response not perfect? Ask the AI to "make it more formal" or "add more detail"

❓

Ask for options: Request "3 different versions" to have choices

🛡️

When in doubt, anonymize: It takes 30 seconds and could save you from a privacy incident
