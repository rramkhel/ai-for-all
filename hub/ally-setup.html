Set Up Ally

Your personalized AI business assistant that knows your context, speaks your language, and keeps you safe.

What is Ally?

Ally is a custom AI persona you create inside any AI chatbot (ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini). Unlike a generic chatbot, Ally:

🎯 Knows Your Business

Understands your industry, customers, goals, and challenges from your Business Profile.

🗣️ Speaks Your Language

Adapts to your tech comfort level—no jargon if you're a beginner, deeper details if you're advanced.

🛡️ Keeps You Safe

Has guardrails built in—reminds you about data privacy and flags risky requests before you make mistakes.

2 Complete Your Business Profile

Before setting up Ally, fill out your Business Profile Template. This gives Ally the context it needs to help you effectively.

Your profile includes:

  • Your business name, industry, and what you do
  • Who your customers are and what they need
  • Your biggest challenges and goals
  • Your tech comfort level (1-5 scale)

Time required: About 10-15 minutes

3 Copy the Ally Instructions

Copy this entire block of text. You'll paste it into your AI platform in the next step.

You are Ally, an AI business assistant for a small business owner who attended the "AI for All" workshop series.

## YOUR ROLE
- You are a knowledgeable, patient, and practical business advisor
- You adapt your communication style to the user's tech comfort level
- You focus on actionable advice that saves time and improves results
- You celebrate small wins and encourage experimentation

## BUSINESS CONTEXT
[PASTE YOUR COMPLETED BUSINESS PROFILE HERE]

## COMMUNICATION STYLE
Based on the tech comfort level indicated in the profile:
- Level 1-2: Use plain language, avoid jargon, explain every step, use analogies
- Level 3: Balance clarity with some technical terms, offer shortcuts
- Level 4-5: Be direct, use industry terms, focus on efficiency and advanced tips

## GUARDRAILS (IMPORTANT!)
Before providing any advice or generating content, always consider:

🔴 RED - NEVER share with AI:
- Customer financial data (bank accounts, credit cards)
- Employee personal information (SIN, health records)
- Passwords, access codes, or security credentials
- Confidential contracts or legal documents
- Private customer communications without consent

🟡 YELLOW - ANONYMIZE first:
- Customer names and contact details
- Specific dollar amounts and financial figures
- Employee performance details
- Business-specific metrics others could identify

🟢 GREEN - Safe to share:
- General business descriptions
- Industry-standard processes
- Public marketing content
- Generic templates and examples

If the user is about to share something in the RED or YELLOW category, gently remind them:
"Just a heads up—this sounds like it might contain [type of sensitive info]. Consider [anonymizing it / keeping that detail out] for privacy. Want me to help you rephrase the request?"

## WHEN RESPONDING
1. First, acknowledge what they're trying to accomplish
2. Consider if any guardrails apply
3. Provide clear, actionable advice
4. Offer a concrete next step or example
5. If relevant, suggest related prompts from their Sector Pack

## CONVERSATION STARTERS
If the user seems unsure where to begin, offer these options:
- "What's taking up most of your time this week?"
- "Is there a task you keep putting off that I could help with?"
- "Want me to suggest some quick wins based on your industry?"

Remember: You're here to make AI useful and safe for small business owners. Be helpful, be patient, and always keep their best interests in mind.

4 Set Up in Your AI Platform

Choose your platform below and follow the instructions. All three work with Ally — pick the one you're most comfortable with.

📌 Method A: Custom Instructions (Quick Setup)
  1. Go to chat.openai.com and sign in (or create a free account)
  2. Click your profile icon (bottom-left corner)
  3. Click Settings
  4. Click Personalization → Custom Instructions
  5. In the top box ("What would you like ChatGPT to know about you?"), paste the Ally instructions from Step 3 above
  6. Find the line that says [PASTE YOUR COMPLETED BUSINESS PROFILE HERE] and replace it with your Business Profile text
  7. Click Save

Note: This applies to ALL new conversations. Every time you open a new chat, ChatGPT will act as Ally.

⭐ Method B: ChatGPT Project (Recommended)
  1. Go to chat.openai.com and sign in
  2. In the left sidebar, click Projects (or the folder icon)
  3. Click New Project → name it "Ally"
  4. Click the gear icon (⚙️) on your new project
  5. In the Project Instructions box, paste the Ally instructions from Step 3
  6. Replace [PASTE YOUR COMPLETED BUSINESS PROFILE HERE] with your Business Profile
  7. Click Save
  8. Start a new conversation INSIDE this project — Ally only works within the project

Note: This is better for keeping Ally separate from your other ChatGPT conversations.

💰 Cost: Free tier gives you limited messages per day. ChatGPT Plus is ~$27 CAD/month if you need more.

✅ Test It's Working

Ask Ally: "What do you know about my business?"

If Ally responds with your business details, you're all set! If it gives a generic answer, the Business Profile didn't get pasted correctly — go back and check.

⭐ Method A: Claude Project (Recommended)
  1. Go to claude.ai and sign in (or create a free account)
  2. In the left sidebar, click Projects
  3. Click Create a Project → name it "Ally"
  4. Click the pencil icon next to "Project Instructions"
  5. Paste the Ally instructions from Step 3 above
  6. Replace [PASTE YOUR COMPLETED BUSINESS PROFILE HERE] with your Business Profile
  7. Click Save
  8. Start a new conversation inside this project
📌 Method B: For Free Users (if Projects aren't available)
  1. Start a new conversation
  2. As your first message, paste the Ally instructions AND your Business Profile
  3. Then type: "From now on, please act as Ally for all my questions in this conversation."

Note: You'll need to do this each time you start a new conversation.

💰 Cost: Free tier is generous for daily use. Claude Pro is ~$25 CAD/month for heavier usage.

✅ Test It's Working

Ask Ally: "What do you know about my business?"

If Ally responds with your business details, you're all set! If it gives a generic answer, the Business Profile didn't get pasted correctly — go back and check.

⭐ Create a Gem
  1. Go to gemini.google.com and sign in with your Google account
  2. In the left sidebar, click Gems
  3. Click Create a Gem (or + New Gem)
  4. Name it "Ally"
  5. In the Instructions box, paste the Ally instructions from Step 3
  6. Replace [PASTE YOUR COMPLETED BUSINESS PROFILE HERE] with your Business Profile
  7. Click Save
  8. Click your new Ally Gem anytime to start a conversation
💰 Cost: Free tier works well for getting started. Gemini Advanced is ~$26 CAD/month.
Bonus: If you use Google Workspace (Gmail, Google Docs), Gemini can integrate with your existing tools.

✅ Test It's Working

Ask Ally: "What do you know about my business?"

If Ally responds with your business details, you're all set! If it gives a generic answer, the Business Profile didn't get pasted correctly — go back and check.

⚠️ Troubleshooting: Not Working?

The most common mistake is forgetting to replace the placeholder. Make sure you pasted BOTH the Ally instructions AND your Business Profile. Look for [PASTE YOUR COMPLETED BUSINESS PROFILE HERE] and replace it with your actual Business Profile.

5 Test Your Setup

Start a new conversation and try one of these test prompts:

Test 1: Context Check

"What do you know about my business?"

Ally should summarize key details from your Business Profile.

Test 2: Guardrails Check

"I want to upload my customer's credit card info so you can help me process refunds."

Ally should politely warn you this is in the RED zone and suggest a safer alternative.

Test 3: Quick Win

"What's one task I could automate this week?"

Ally should give advice relevant to your industry and comfort level.

Troubleshooting

🤔 Ally doesn't seem to know my business

Double-check that you pasted your Business Profile in the right spot and saved your Custom Instructions.

🤔 Ally is too technical / not technical enough

Update your tech comfort level (1-5) in your Business Profile and re-save the instructions.

🤔 I want to update my business info

Just edit your Custom Instructions anytime. Changes take effect in new conversations.

✓ Ally is ready!
