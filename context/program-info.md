# AI for All — Program Information

## Program Overview

- **Full name:** AI for All: High-Impact AI for SMB Growth
- **Organization:** St. Albert & District Chamber of Commerce (SACC)
- **Duration:** 16 months (February 2026 – June 2027)
- **Sessions:** 15 total, once per month
- **Format:** In-person with hybrid elements and recordings
- **Session length:** 90 minutes
- **Summer break:** July–August 2026

## Target Audience

- St. Albert area businesses
- 87% are micro-businesses (<5 employees)
- Primarily owner-operators
- Sectors: professional services, retail, construction/trades, manufacturing
- More intermediate AI experience than initially assumed

## Key Scheduling Insights (from survey)

- **Preferred day:** Thursday (most popular by far)
- **Best time slots:** Lunch (12:00-1:30 PM) on Tue/Wed/Thu works best for micro-business owners
- **Mid-morning (9:30-11:00 AM):** Strong demand, underutilized in initial planning
- **Format preference:** In-person with recordings available

## Key Concepts

- **"Sous chef" analogy:** AI as a skilled helper that enhances your work (you're still the chef)
- **"Intern" mindset:** Treat AI like a capable but new employee who needs clear direction
- **Content packets:** Take-home resource bundles given at each session
