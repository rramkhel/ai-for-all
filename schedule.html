Option 1: Spaced Repeats (Jeff's Original Schedule)

Baseline

ðŸ“Š Rationale

Sessions repeated weeks or months apart to provide maximum flexibility for businesses with seasonal travel patterns (winter holidays south) and to allow adequate time for implementation and practice between learning modules. This approach spreads the workload evenly throughout the year and reduces instructor fatigue from back-to-back identical sessions. Provides breathing room for businesses to test tools and see ROI before advancing to next topic.

Momentum Builder
