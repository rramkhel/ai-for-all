Monthly Workshop Schedule

16-Month Program

Rationale

One focused workshop per month allows for deeper implementation and practice between sessions. This approach spreads the workload evenly throughout the program and reduces overwhelm for busy business owners. The 16-month timeline (Feb 2026 - June 2027) provides sustainable learning with breathing room to test tools and see ROI before advancing to the next topic.
